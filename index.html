About Pacific NorthwestAgroforest

Pacific Northwest Agroforest develops diversified agroforestry systems that advance ecological restoration, climate resilience, and sustainable economic development. The organization emphasizes perennial agriculture, biodiversity enhancement, and value-added land-based production models suited to the temperate ecosystems of western North America.

Pacific Northwest Agroforest recognizes the longstanding stewardship traditions and enduring presence of Indigenous communities connected to the landscapes where we work. Our approach emphasizes ecological restoration, reciprocity, and responsible long-term stewardship.
